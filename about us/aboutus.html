About Revour

Connected homes, connecting zindagi!

Founded in Mar 2019, Revour presents next generation Smart LEDs, Fans, Solar Kits, MCBs and Switches. Revour aims to redefine future consumer choice through Automation, IoT and Smart Apps.

With a strong expanding dealer network, Revour has created reach in over 2,000 retail outlets across UP, MH, UK and HY and have crossed over 200 SKU variants and 250,000 unit sales within a short time of launch.

Revour is an Oriano founded venture. Founded in June 2015, Oriano was named “4th fastest growing technology company in India” by Deloitte Fast50 in Oct 2018.

Oriano with revenues of $40 million has raised $5 million of Equity & Debt from SIDBI Ventures (backed by DFID, UK and SIDBI) and Caspian Impact Investments.
